Become a Five-Paw Chef

Description

Would you like it if you had to eat the same dry food for every single meal? Your dog shouldn't have to either! Make sure to cook for your flock three times a day to keep them happy and healthy.

Ingredients

  • Bone marrow, frozen (60 pieces)
  • Steak, any cut, raw (4oz per guest)

Steps

  1. Provide one piece of bone marrow every day as a high-value treat
  2. Cut up the raw streak into equal portions for each dog
  3. Deliver on a silver platter
  4. Repeat three times a day or mealtime per your feeding schedule
