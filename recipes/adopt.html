'Who Rescued Who', Am I Right?

Description

Let's face it: you're lonely, not that fun to be around, and quite frankly not the best or worst person in the world. Well, luckily there's a solution that even benefits a most precious and innocent breed of dog: the German Shepherd. That's right, baby! We're on a mission to rescue these magnificant souls from the evil bars they live behind.

Now, let's get ready to break our soon-to-be friends out of doggy prison!

Ingredients

Before we become a doggy-fugitive camp, we must attain some key items such as:

  • A big heart
  • An even bigger yard
  • Lots of patience
  • Pen and paper
  • Internet connection
  • A lint roller, or two...

Steps

In order to bust out our friends, research and planning is essential!

  1. Establish a secure online connection.
  2. Search every single rescue/shelter in your area for German Shepherds.
  3. Make a record of each one you find.
  4. Contact each location to get more information about the doggy prisoners.
  5. Sort your data according to age and time served.
  6. Map out each shelter's floorplan, and when you're ready, start the mission!
  7. Ambush the shelters by setting all of the animals free.
  8. Grab the German Shepherds, making sure seniors are first out followed by those with the most time served
  9. Book it to your car to go home!
  10. Check out your new fleet of loyal, loving, and highly-intelligent soldiers!
  11. Pat yourself on the back for saving so many lives!
